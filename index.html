About Dubai to the Stars

Pioneering the future of space tourism since 2030

Our Vision

Dubai to the Stars was founded with a singular vision: to make space travel accessible, safe, and luxurious for civilians. As the UAE's premier space tourism company, we leverage decades of aerospace innovation and Dubai's legacy of turning ambitious dreams into reality.

Our Journey

What began as a bold initiative by the UAE Space Agency has evolved into the world's leading commercial space travel enterprise. Our fleet of spacecraft combines cutting-edge technology with unparalleled comfort, while our orbital and planetary accommodations redefine luxury beyond Earth.

Safety & Innovation

Safety is at the core of everything we do. Our vessels and habitats undergo rigorous testing and exceed all international space safety standards. We continuously push the boundaries of what's possible in space tourism, investing heavily in research and development to enhance the space travel experience.
